# Software Engineer Intern - PawsProfile

## Company Description
At PawsProfile, we’re redefining pet care by building a one-stop platform that addresses every need of pet parents. From expert veterinary services to personalized health tracking, grooming, training, and more, our goal is to make pet care both convenient and top-notch. As a fast-growing startup, we’re passionate about creating solutions that truly make a difference in the lives of pets and their families.

## Role Description
We’re on the hunt for Software Engineer Interns who are excited about working in a startup environment, solving real-world problems, and contributing to building an impactful product from the ground up. This is a hands-on role where you’ll work directly with the founder to develop and launch the core platform, including our mobile app and website.

## Key Responsibilities
- Collaborate closely with the founder to design and implement product features.
- Take ownership of specific modules in the development of the mobile app and website.
- Work on front-end, back-end, or full-stack development, depending on your role.
- Participate in daily progress updates and regular team meetings to ensure alignment.
- Use modern tools and frameworks to design user-friendly interfaces and scalable systems.

## Roles Available
1. **Front-End Developer**:
   - Develop responsive, visually appealing interfaces using React.js, Tailwind CSS, and similar frameworks.
   - Optimize user experiences with interactive designs and seamless functionality.
2. **Back-End Developer**:
   - Build scalable APIs and manage databases using Node.js, Express, and MongoDB or other tools.
   - Ensure smooth integration between the front-end and back-end systems.
3. **Full-Stack Developer**:
   - Take charge of both front-end and back-end development to deliver end-to-end solutions.
   - Work with React, Node.js, Tailwind CSS, MongoDB, and other technologies.
4. **UI/UX Designer**:
   - Create wireframes, mockups, and user flows for the app and website.
   - Ensure designs are intuitive, modern, and user-centric.

## Technologies You’ll Work With
- **Front-End**: React.js, Tailwind CSS, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript/TypeScript
- **Back-End**: Node.js, Express.js, REST APIs, MongoDB
- **UI/UX Tools**: Figma, Adobe XD, or similar design tools
- **Mobile Development**: React Native (preferred but not mandatory)
- **Version Control**: Git/GitHub
- **Deployment**: AWS, Netlify, or Firebase (as needed)
